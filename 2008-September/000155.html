Summary: SLiM can't handle logins with secure passwords

Details: SLiM can't handle logins with secure passwords.

I've just installed SLiM and tried to login. Instead of logging into my DE, it just beeped and reloaded its login screen again.

After changing my users' passwords to the far too short and far too insecure password "testpw", SLiM logged me into my DE.

So I think, that the password field/variable is far too short and/or doesn't accept every possible character.

The password field/variable and/or its content should not be limited in any way. This needs urgently be fixed.
